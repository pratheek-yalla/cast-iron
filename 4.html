• Step 3: Grinding of sample

  • Grinding:

  • Diamond on rigid discs is recommended for fine grinding of most cast irons, as it keeps the samples very flat and does not leave the graphite in relief.
  • Hard white cast irons and austempered ductile irons can be plane/ fine ground with diamond.
  • Soft and medium-hard cast irons with a ferritic, austenitic or pearlitic matrix should be plane ground with silicon carbide foil/paper and fine ground with diamond.
